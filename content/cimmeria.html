Cimmeria

/sɪˈmɪəriə/

Underneath the opulent, the divine, and the lustrous,

Unbefitting our esteemed, righteous, and faithful path,

Unfurls a world of ill-fated tales, inverted ideals, and imitation practice.

-

When the tides of time churn and the clock strikes the 13th hour, a king of yore hath revived.

Whence comes this king is a matter of grave importance, for who is it that dictates the order of our world?

-

Come hither, come witness. The age of enlightenment is upon us. We must seek truth, lest mystery enshroud us all...
