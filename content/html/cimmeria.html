Cimmeria

/sɪˈmɪəriə/

Underneath the opulent, the divine, and the lustrous,

unbefitting our esteemed, righteous, and faithful path,

unfurls a world of the ill-fated, the inverted, and the imitated.

-

The tides of time churn and the 13th month arrives,

a sovereign of yore wakes thus the dreamer mourns.

Whence comes this sovereign is a matter of little importance,

for who is it that dictates the order of our world?

-

Come hither, come witness,

the age of enlightenment is upon us.

Make haste, make due,

the city of dawn shall burn in lieu.
